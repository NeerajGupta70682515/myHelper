FAQS

Can you provide current account ?

HBF Nidhi Ltd shall not open any current account with its members;

Nidhi Company is a type of but is not the substitute to fully fledged NBFC or a Bank. Hence, it is not allowed to open the current account.

Can Hbf Nidhi Limited offer Lockers to their members ?

Yes, they can provide lockers to members only.

Is HBF nidhi limited is bank ? what is nidhi mean ?

No, we are not bank. "Nidhi" is a Hindi word, which means finance or fund. Nidhi" means a company which has been incorporated as a Nidhi with the object of developing the habit of thrift and reserve funds amongst its members, receiving deposits, and lending to its members only for their mutual benefit.We cannot run any other business in its name.

Where you registered & is RBI involved with your organization ?

We registered with Ministry of corporate affairs (Govt of India). Hbf Nidhi Limited follow all guidelines from RBI, but we don't require any license from RBI.

Can I buy share in your Org?

Yes, you can buy share in our organisation. If you are not a member than first you have to be a member of our organisation.

Why should I open a saving account with your org.

Hbf Nidhi ltd never treat anyone as a member/customer/employee, we believe you all are our family. So we try to resolve ground level difficulties, we do special care of senior citizen and ladies , A person can open saving account with just 100 rs .. we provide 6% interest rate on our saving account. We also provide Doorstep services to collect funds & etc.

How much fund you have to reserve as a CRR or balance

  • HBF NIDHI Limited min maintain 10% of Total collection amount.
  • For ex Hbf nidhi limited.
  • Collect : 2 cr saving account ,15 cr fixed depost ,3 cr Reccuring deposit.
  • Min CRR mainitain every time : 2cr.

Can you provide cheque book ?

No, as we told you we are not a bank so we can't provide cheque book.

Can you provide debit card ?

Yes, we can provide debit card on behalf of Bank. We will come soon with debit cards on behalf of Banks.

Can I transfer my fund to other of my account in bank or nidhi company and can I transfer to any of my relative or friends account ?

Yes you can transfer the money, once you will request to transfer, your relationship manager will contact you for verification .After verification your funds will be transferred.
