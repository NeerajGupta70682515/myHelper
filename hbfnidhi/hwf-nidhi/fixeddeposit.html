Nomination

Nomination facility available for deposits. There can be only one Nominee for a deposit account whether held singly or jointly. Applicants can make nomination by filling up the prescribed form. The nomination details can be changed during the subsistence of the account relationship by filling up the prescribed form.

No foreclosure shall be allowed within 3 months of deposit;
  • No foreclosure shall be allowed within 3 months of deposit.
  • No interest shall be allowed for foreclosure between three and six months of deposits. Only principal amount will be refunded.
  • Interest shall be allowed at the Savings Bank rate payable by the HBF Nidhi LTD for foreclosure between six and twelve months.
  • If foreclosure is opted for after twelve months of deposit the interest payable on such deposits would be reduced by 2% from the contracted rate of interest payable for such period for which the deposits have remained with the company.
Maturity terms
  • No foreclosure shall be allowed within 3 months of deposit.
  • No interest shall be paid on foreclosure between three and six months of deposits. Only principal amount will be refunded.
  • Interest shall be allowed at 4% rate payable by the HBF Nidhi LTD for foreclosure between six and twelve months.
  • If foreclosure is opted for after twelve months of deposit the interest payable on such deposits would be 6% interest payable for such period for which the deposits have remained with the company.
  • Premature withdrawal is not available if you choose Non-Breakable FD scheme
  • Company can have deducted 1% from principle amount as a foreclosure or administration charges
