Professional Finance & Banking Program

What is the HBF Professional Finance & Banking Program?

This is a program under which talented finance professionals, they can manage Banking or accounting for 180 days to enable them to become dynamic banking professionals. 15-Day Residential Program 165-Day On-the-Job Training with Performance and Mentoring.

What would be the Job Role?

The role would be to retain customers by building relationships.

Required Qualification:

For Graduates & Post Graduates (50% marks compulsory) less than 28 years of age.

Professional Doorstep Banking & Marketing

What is the HBF Professional Doorstep Banking & Marketing Program?

This is a program under which talented sales and service professionals will be imparted functional training and groomed for 90 days to enable them to become dynamic banking professionals.

What would be the Job Role?

The role would be to acquire new customers and cross sales with customer.

Required Qualification:

For Graduates & Post Graduates (50% marks compulsory) less than 28 years of age.
